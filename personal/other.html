Other Considerations

One of the unseen perils of a survival situation is the mental stress that comes from not knowing when you will be rescued and from the inevitable boredom that comes along with waiting for help. What ever keeps you occupied at home can be useful additions to your safety kit. Examples of this include:

Deck of cards Books to read Books to color Crosswords Notepad and pens Knitting Crochet

By having something to do while you are waiting for help, you can keep your mind sharp and be able to keep the boredom to a minimum.
